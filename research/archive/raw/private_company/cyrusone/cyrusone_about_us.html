About CyrusOne

Delivering State-of-the-Art Digital Infrastructure

Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, CyrusOne operates more than 60 data centers across North America, EMEA, and APAC.

Why Choose CyrusOne?

Global Leadership in Digital Infrastructure

CyrusOne is a premier data center owner, developer, and operator, providing advanced digital infrastructure solutions across the globe. We specialize in comprehensive solutions for hyperscale and global enterprises, enabling organizations to meet their business and sustainability objectives. Our facilities support demanding AI-driven applications and high-density deployments while providing the flexibility to adapt to rapidly evolving technology requirements. Through build-to-suit, colocation and interconnection solutions, we deliver infrastructure that scales with our customers' needs.

Learn More

Advanced Design for Tomorrow's Technologies

We design and build advanced data centers capable of supporting all deployments and emerging technologies while prioritizing sustainability goals. Our engineering and design services encompass comprehensive power planning, strategic floor layouts, connectivity optimization, and detailed implementation scheduling. Through systematic measurement and analysis, we continuously enhance our facilities and operations to meet evolving technological demands.

Learn More

Hyperscale and Large Enterprises Trust CyrusOne

Leading hyperscale companies and global enterprises partner with us to develop scalable data center infrastructure that meets their most demanding technical requirements. Our team brings decades of experience in engineering, construction, and operations, guided by our core values of commitment, accountability, respect, and excellence. This expertise, combined with our focus on sustainability, enables us to deliver solutions that drive long-term success for our customers.
