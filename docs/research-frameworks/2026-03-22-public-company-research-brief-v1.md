# Public Company Research Brief v1

Date: March 22, 2026

## Purpose

Turn execution-ready public handoff rows into filing-backed company research
briefs for the next human research pass.

## Why This Exists

The execution handoff is sufficient for queueing.

It is not sufficient for actual company work because it does not yet put:

1. thesis
2. filing-backed support counts
3. top excerpts
4. concrete research questions

into one object.

## Inputs

1. public execution handoff
2. bounded entity filing support

## Output

Each brief row contains:

1. canonical entity
2. resolved issuer
3. ticker
4. market theme
5. role label
6. thesis
7. filing evidence summary
8. top supporting excerpts
9. role-specific research questions
10. catalysts and invalidations

## Current Use

This is the first company-level research object for the default U.S.-executable
queue.

The immediate target names are:

1. `GE Vernova`
2. `Eaton`
